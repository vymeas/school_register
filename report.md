# 🏫 School Management & Payment System: Reports & Printing Specifications

This document outlines the required reports and professional printing features for the school management system, with a primary focus on student payments and classroom tracking.

---

## 📊 1. System Reports Directory



### 1.1 Classroom Reports
Focuses on physical/virtual room management and current attendance.
* **Report Summary Classroom:** * **Data:** List of all active classrooms, Grade Level, assigned Teacher, Study Turn (Morning/Evening), and total student count.
    * **Purpose:** High-level capacity and resource overview.
* **Report Detail Classroom (Class Roster):** * **Data:** Specific classroom view (e.g., "Grade 10A - Morning"). Lists full student roster, contact info, and immediate payment status (Paid/Unpaid).
    * **Purpose:** Daily management and attendance for teachers.

### 1.2 Student Reports
Tracks individual student lifecycles and enrollment.
* **Report Summary Student:** * **Data:** Total active students grouped by Grade or Study Turn. Active vs. Dropped Out metrics.
    * **Purpose:** Monitor overall school enrollment growth.
* **Report Detail Student (Student Profile):** * **Data:** Individual student info, assigned classroom, Study Turn, current Tuition Plan, and complete payment history.
    * **Purpose:** Dispute resolution and detailed record-keeping.

### 1.3 Term / Grade Reports
Tracks academic levels and their performance.
* **Report Summary Term/Grade:** * **Data:** List of all grades (Grade 10, 11, etc.) and total student count per grade.
    * **Purpose:** Identify the most populated academic levels.
* **Report Detail Term/Grade:** * **Data:** Classrooms within a specific grade, assigned teachers, and total revenue generated by that grade.
    * **Purpose:** Resource allocation and marketing focus.

### 1.4 Teacher Reports
Manages staff directories and workloads.
* **Report Summary Teacher:** * **Data:** Master list of all teachers, contact info, subjects, and total classrooms managed.
    * **Purpose:** Quick administrative staff directory.
* **Report Detail Teacher (Teacher Schedule):** * **Data:** Specific teacher's workload, detailing classrooms, Grades, and turns (Morning/Evening) taught.
    * **Purpose:** Prevent schedule conflicts and double-booking.

### 1.5 Payment Reports (⭐ Core Priority)
Financial tracking based on time-based tuition plans (1, 3, 6, 12 months) and Expiry Dates.
* **Report Summary Payment (Revenue Over Time):** * **Data:** Total money collected (Today/Weekly/Monthly), broken down by Tuition Plan type (e.g., revenue from 1-Month vs. 3-Month plans).
    * **Purpose:** Financial health tracking for school owners.
* **Report Detail Payment (Transaction Log):** * **Data:** Chronological list of all payments. Includes Date, Student, Classroom, Amount, and Plan.
    * **Purpose:** Accounting reconciliation and auditing.
* **Report Detail Debt / Overdue (Action Report):** * **Data:** Targeted list of students whose `Expiry_Date` has passed. Shows Student Name, Classroom, Phone Number, and Days Overdue.
    * **Purpose:** Direct collection and parent follow-up.

## 🖨️ 2. Professional Printing & Export Features

To ensure the system is ready for real-world school administration, the following printing features must be implemented.



### 2.1 Essential Export Options
* **Export to PDF:** Locks data and layout for official, uneditable printing.
* **Export to Excel / CSV:** Available on all Payment and Summary reports for the accounting department.

### 2.2 Print-Optimized Layouts (`@media print`)
* **Hide UI:** Automatically hide navigation menus, sidebars, and buttons when printing.
* **Ink-Saving Mode:** Convert dark backgrounds to white, text to black, and remove unnecessary background colors.
* **Table Pagination:** Ensure long tables (like Class Rosters) break cleanly across pages without cutting off rows.

### 2.3 Official Headers & Footers
* **Dynamic Headers:** Must include the School Logo, School Name, and the exact Title of the report.
* **Dynamic Footers:** Must include Timestamp (Date/Time generated), User ID (who printed it), and Page Numbers (Page X of Y).

### 2.4 Official Payment Receipts
* **Data Included:** Student Name, Classroom, Payment Date, Amount Paid, Tuition Plan selected, and the new **Expiry Date**.
* **Signatures:** Blank signature lines at the bottom for "Cashier Signature" and "Student/Parent Signature" for legal proof of payment.

### 2.5 Batch Printing
* **Functionality:** Allow admins to select multiple students from the "Debt / Overdue Report" and print all reminder notices or invoices at once in a single, combined PDF file.
